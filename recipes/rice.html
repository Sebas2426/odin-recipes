Rice witch chicken

Description

This One Pot Chicken and Rice recipe is an easy-to-make, delicious and family-friendly meal. I make it with simple pantry items and any available vegetables

Ingredients

  • Onion and garlic
  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Broth
  • Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the onions
  2. Add the cubed chicken
  3. Add any vegetables you're using and rice
  4. Add chicken broth or water
  5. Allow the one pot chicken and rice to rest for 5-10 minutes
